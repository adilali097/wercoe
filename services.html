Company Formation & Legal Structuring


Company Formation

  • Limited Liability Company (LLC): LLCs in the UAE are popular for their easy setup and incentives. They typically require 51% local ownership, but recent changes allow for 100% foreign ownership in certain sectors. We offer a secure nominee structure to handle local sponsorship and ensure smooth operation.
  • Foreign Branch Office: A Branch Office in Dubai can be 100% foreign-owned with a local service agent. It operates under the parent company’s name and is restricted to the same activities as the parent company.
  • Trade Representative Office: This office promotes the parent company in the UAE but cannot conduct sales or trade. It requires a local sponsor and a refundable bank guarantee of AED50,000, which cannot be used as working capital.
  • Professional Service License: Designed for businesses owned by professionals like doctors, engineers, and lawyers, this license requires a local sponsor but allows full ownership by the professionals. Engineering firms must include a UAE National partner.
